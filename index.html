our Experience

Visiting Bali is a truly unforgettable experience that will leave you with lasting memories of the trip! This Indonesian island is known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant atmosphere. Whether you're looking to relax on a tropical beach, explore ancient temples, taste the exquisite cuisines, or experience the island's famous nightlife, Bali has something for everyone.

A land of tropical beaches, cute koalas, endearing kangaroos, Aboriginal culture, and lush rainforests, Australia is a traveller's dream destination with experiences that you will cherish for eternity! From adventure sports like water jetpacking and skydiving across Gold Coast’s pristine beaches, to scuba diving in the Great Barrier Reef, there is an abundance of activities that will make your heart race and pump up the adrenaline.

Step into the world of majestic mountains and warm traditions in the heart of the Alps – Tyrol, Austria. Whether it is hiking or rock climbing in the summers or skiing and tobogganing in the snowy mountains, there is something to do all the year round. For travellers looking to explore beyond Vienna and Salzburg, this is the place to enjoy an experience of a lifetime.

Explore the outdoors and breathe in the fresh air at the Black Forest area of Germany which has something for every type of traveller! Known for its dark conifer forests, enchanting villages, hiking trails along the Triberg Waterfalls, thermal spas and the cuckoo clocks, this place is soaked in its rich tradition and culture and is bound to leave you with a lifetime of memories!

Regarded as South Africa’s most beautiful region, the Garden Route begins about four hours outside of Cape Town and stretches across one of the most popular and diverse coastlines in the country. A temperate landscape of family friendly beaches, lakes and lagoons, diverse vegetation and wildlife, mountains and forests, and a simple, scenic, ocean hugging highway that guarantees the ultimate adventure!

If you are looking for a rich cultural and historical experience, all while being surrounded by natural beauty and grand palaces, then head to Rajasthan in India that offers an array of exciting activities and breath-taking sights! You will not only get to witness the architectural marvel of the numerous palaces, forts and temples with their intricate and colourful frescoes and carvings

A blend of traditional culture and modern technology, Japan is a land of ancient shrines and temples that coexist together with its bustling cities and world-renowned developments! Experience its stunning landscapes and its unique culture including the famous tea ceremonies and the art of flower arrangement, Ikebana

A trip to the Southern Hemisphere is incomplete without travelling to the country known for its Maori tradition and geothermal mud spas – New Zealand! Whether it is adventure travel with activities such as bungee jumping or skydiving, or scenic escapades through Gondola rides and travelling on the Tranzalpine – there is something for everyone!

Indulge in a rich and diverse travel experience in Northern Italy, which is known for its rich history and culture, natural beauty and enthralling outdoor adventures! Explore the ‘City of Canals’ Venice, the fashion capital Milan with its world-renowned museums, historic cathedrals and the famous ‘Last Supper’ painting by Leonardo da Vinci, and Lake Como with its sparkling blue waters, lush green hills and picturesque towns.

Take a trip to this culturally rich and historically significant country which was at the heart of the Ottoman and Byzantine Empires! Enjoy activities like a hot air balloon ride in Cappadocia or a curative dip in the medicinal thermal hot springs of Pamukkale. Spend a memorable night in a cave hotel in Goreme for a one-of-a-kind experience.
