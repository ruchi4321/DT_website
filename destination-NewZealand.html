New Zealand

Countries covered

Get ready to be amazed by the unbelievable scenery of New Zealand. This country's beautiful mountains, lakes, and fjords have earned it international acclaim. The South Island is home to snow-capped summits, untouched glaciers, and spectacular hiking paths of the Southern Alps. Milford Sound is a must-see, a breathtaking fjord with towering cliffs and flowing waterfalls.

For animal enthusiasts, New Zealand is the ideal vacation spot because of its lot of rare and unusual species. The kiwi bird, for example, is a cute species to meet due to its distinct look and nocturnal activities. The coastal areas are the best places to see dolphins and whales in their natural habitat. Remember the sheep, too! You may expect to see many sheep in New Zealand, home to a large sheep population.

The Best of New Zealand

Thrilling
Adventures

New Zealand is a paradise for thrill-seekers, with many exciting adventure activities. Queenstown is the most popular destination in the world for extreme sports. It includes bungee jumping, skydiving, and jet boating. You may kayak, sail, or swim with dolphins at the Bay of Islands or Lake Taupo. There are plenty of places to go hiking, mountain biking, and even heli-skiing.

Captivating
Cities

New Zealand's vibrant cities combine cutting-edge design with a laid-back lifestyle. Auckland, the capital, is a cultural melting pot with a busy waterfront, good restaurants, and a vibrant arts scene. Wellington, the capital, has several museums, galleries, and a thriving film industry. Christchurch's South Island floral gardens make it attractive. Also, get the chance to see the city's reconstruction efforts after a terrible earthquake.

our Destination

Bali is the westernmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands. A mountain range stretches from west to east of Bali - a zone of high volcanic activity. The island is famous for traditional wooden sculpture and painting, as well as batik painting on fabric. Balinese dances are theatrical performances, the general meaning of which is based on the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Wellington

Queenstown

Learn more
Bungee jumping from the Kawarau Bridge is an unforgettable experience. Enjoy breathtaking views of Lake Wakatipu from atop Bob's Peak, accessible through a picturesque gondola ride. Take a sail in Milford Sound and let the breathtaking scenery take your breath away.

Rotorua

Learn more
Visit a traditional Maori village and partake in a hangi feast to fully immerse yourself in the culture. Visit TePuia to witness the spectacular Pohutu Geyser and other geothermal attractions. The Polynesian Spa's hot mineral pools and spa treatments will help you unwind and refresh.

Franz Josef Glacier

Learn more
Visit the South Island's West Coast to see the breathtaking Franz Josef Glacier, a sight unlike any other in the world. Explore the glacier's cold landscape and impressive ice formations with a guided tour or a helicopter ride. Relax in the soothing hot pools and enjoy the natural splendor of the nearby jungle.
