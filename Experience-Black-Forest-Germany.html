Known for its dark conifer forests, enchanting villages, hiking trails along the Triberg Waterfalls, thermal spas and the cuckoo clocks, this place is soaked in its rich tradition and culture and is bound to leave you with a lifetime of memories!

Enjoy the Rush of Adrenaline at Hirschgrund Zipline

If you are an adrenaline junkie, then visit the Hirschgrund Zipline, which consists of a 570-metre-long and 83-metre-high zipline which is the longest in Germany! The area, which offers spectacular views of the Kinzig Valley, is a combination of 7 connected ziplines that lead you over steep rock slopes and valleys, hills and creeks and provide a splendid aerial view of the Black Forest region!

The Best of Black Forest

Explore the World of Cuckoo Clocks at Triberg

Start your trip at Triberg im Schwarzwald, an old, picturesque and charming town. Hike up to the Triberg Waterfalls, which are 163 metres in height and fall into the Gutach river. There are also some great hikes in the Ravenna gorge through waterfalls, water mills, and a 37-metre-high Ravenna Bridge- the viaduct of the Höllental Railway.After your excursions, relax and indulge in the rich taste of a Black Forest Gateau, a specialty cake of this place made with black cherries, chocolate sponge and whipped cream. Continue on your journey at the open-air Black Forest Museum where you can learn more about the local crafts, traditional costumes and culture of the region and how it has transformed over the years.

Lake Titisee Rejuvenate at the Badeparadies Water Park

Take a boat ride on Lake Titisee’s crystal-blue waters, one of Black Forest’s tourism hotspots, and lose yourself in the scenic beauty of the area. You can sunbathe, swim or ice-skate in order to relax and enjoy the outdoors. You can also walk around the lake or hike up to the Hochfirst mountain at a height of about 350 metres, for panoramic views of the region. Next head to Badeparadies Water Park which combines three themed areas – Palais Vital offering a variety of themed saunas, Palm Oasis which will allow you to relax on the numerous water jet lounges surrounded by palm trees, and the Galaxy Schwarzwald for the thrill seekers with its 25 water attractions.

Enjoy a Fun-filled Family Day at Europa Adventure Park

Enjoy a day trip to the Europa Park Resort in Rust which has the largest theme park in Germany. Split into 15 themed areas, the Park has over 100 attractions, 13 roller coasters, fairytale worlds including the interactive Rapunzel Tower, as well as indoor attractions such as the Yullbe virtual reality experience. It also houses the Rulantica Water World, with 30 water slides, 2 pool bars, 3 saunas and other attractions in its 14 themed areas. Have a blast with your family and let your inner child out for a day of fun and adventure!

Indulge in some R&R at Baden Baden Caracella Spa

Enjoy a day of relaxation and rejuvenate your senses at the Caracalla Therme which is located in the heart of Baden-Baden. This spa, which is spread over more than 4000 sq. metres, will help you relax in the hot thermal waters of its numerous pools, rejuvenate you in its Roman-styled saunas, and help revive your senses through massages and treatments in its Wellness Lounge!
