The Best of Dubai and Abu Dhabi

Cultural Oasis
Modern Wonder

Abu Dhabi, the UAE capital, is rich in culture. Abu Dhabi blends history and contemporary with its stunning architecture, historical landmarks, and lively culture. Visit the beautiful Islamic Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque. Visitors marvel at its mosaics and chandeliers. Qasr Al Hosn, Abu Dhabi's oldest stone edifice, provides historical insights for history buffs.

Modern travellers love Dubai's skyscrapers and luxury lifestyles. The Burj Khalifa's observation decks provide stunning city vistas. Shop at the Dubai Mall, one of the world's biggest shopping centres, and discover worldwide brands and entertainment. Dubai's architecture goes beyond the Burj Khalifa. The Palm Jumeirah, a palm-shaped artificial island, has opulent resorts, beautiful beaches, and exciting water parks. Dune smashing, camel riding, and Bedouin entertainment makes desert safaris unique.

Entertainment
and hospitality

Dubai and Abu Dhabi provide world-class hospitality and entertainment. Visitors get unmatched luxury and service in resorts and restaurants. Travellers may enjoy a variety of different cuisines in the UAE. Yas Island in Abu Dhabi is great for entertainment. Yas Waterworld, Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi, and Ferrari World are here. Visit Dubai Marina, a beachfront region featuring restaurants, cafés, and recreational activities. Dhow cruises around Dubai Creek and Arabic coffee at a local café are must-dos.

our Destination

Bali is the westernmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands. A mountain range stretches from west to east of Bali - a zone of high volcanic activity. The island is famous for traditional wooden sculpture and painting, as well as batik painting on fabric. Balinese dances are theatrical performances, the general meaning of which is based on the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Places to visit in Dubai and Abu Dhabi

Here are our best tours for you to enjoy

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

Learn more
  • The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is a work of architectural genius, resplendent in its use of marble, mosaics, and massive chandeliers
  • The peaceful environment here makes this massive mosque one of the best places in the world to worship and think deeply about one's faith.

Burj Khalifa

Learn more
  • Reach new heights at the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest structure.
  • Ride the elevator up to the 148th level and gaze at the spectacular vistas of Dubai's skyline.
  • Spectacular dancing fountains set to music and light up the ground below.

The Palm Jumeirah

Learn more
  • Experience the height of luxury on Palm Jumeirah, a man-made island with a palm tree.
  • Experience luxurious hotels, beautiful beaches, and fine restaurants.
  • Get wet and wild at the Atlantis Aquaventure Waterpark, or relax in the lap of luxury at the Burj Al Arab, a hotel symbolising Dubai's luxury.

Dubai Mall

Learn more
    Dubai Mall offers more than shopping. Luxury brands, foreign boutiques, and unusual stores fill one of the world's biggest malls. Designer brands and exquisite couture will please fashionistas. The mall has several entertainment alternatives. Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo has hundreds of aquatic animals. VR Park, an indoor virtual reality theme park, offers thrills. Watch the Dubai Fountain display as water dances to music and lights.

Dubai Marina

Learn more
Dubai Marina is famous for its skyscrapers, yachts, and lively environment. Stroll down Marina Walk's stylish cafés, restaurants, and stores. Admire the marina's high-rise skyscrapers. Dhow cruises across the marina provide nighttime views of the city's cityscape. The Marina's The Beach is a popular outdoor spot for water activities, relaxing on the beach, and dining with a view. Dubai Marina's glamour, elegance, and tranquil waterfront environment captivate tourists.

Yas Island

Learn more
Beyond the majesty of the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, the other mesmerising attractions in Abu Dhabi include Qasr Al Watan and Yas Island. Learn about the UAE's illustrious past and authoritative government at Qasr Al Watan, and then reward yourself with thrilling activities and exciting shows on Yas Island. There is plenty for tourists of all ages to do and see on Yas Island. The Yas Marina Circuit, the Formula One Abu Dhabi Grand Prix site, will fulfil any racing fan's need for speed. Abu Dhabi has a wide variety of attractions, so tourists may each find something they like.

Qasr Al Watan

Learn more
Qasr Al Watan welcomes visitors and offers them a unique opportunity to explore the UAE's rich heritage, culture, and governance. The palace features several areas that are open to the public, including the Palace Library, the House of Knowledge, the Great Hall, and the Presidential Gifts Exhibition.
