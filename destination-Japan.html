The Best of Japan

Breathtaking
Nature

Japan has stunning scenery. Mount Fuji, a towering volcano, is a must-see. For breathtaking vistas, hike or drive. In April, Japan is a pink paradise with cherry blossoms. Autumn foliage brightens vistas. Hakone and Nikko have several national parks with woods, lakes, and hot springs. Japan's breathtaking scenery will enchant nature enthusiasts.

Rich Cultural
Delights

Japan's cultural history is a major draw for visitors from all over the globe. Everyone will find something they like in Japan. There are long-standing customs to today's most popular music. You will find architecture and insight into Japan's ancient traditions. These are mainly found in Kyoto's many temples and shrines is a highlight for many. Traditional arts, including calligraphy and tea ceremonies, are also available. In addition, visitors of all ages are drawn to Japan for its thriving pop culture.

Key Highlights

Participate in or observe a traditional Japanese tea ceremony, Renting and wearing a kimono, the traditional Japanese garment, can be a memorable experience. Japan is rich in natural hot springs, known as onsens.

Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo DisneySea offer a magical experience with Japanese hospitality and a unique twist on classic Disney themes.

Many regions in Japan host cherry blossom festivals, which are known as "sakura matsuri." These festivals often include food stalls, parades, and performances, making them a popular cultural event.
