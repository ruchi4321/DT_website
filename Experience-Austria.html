For travellers looking to explore beyond Vienna and Salzburg, this is the place to enjoy an experience of a lifetime. Visit the romantic Disney castle or feel the experience of flying with a walk on the Highline 179. For thrill seekers and adventure junkies there are places like Area47 and the Alpine Coaster. And for all the cinema enthusiasts, it is the land of James Bond where the spy performed some extraordinary stunts in the movie ‘Spectre’. Come soak up this travel experience with your family and friends and leave with your hearts full!

Neuschwanstein Castle the Romantic Disney Castle

Start your journey at the Neuschwanstein Castle located only a few kilometres from the Tyrolean border. This world-famous royal castle of the Bavarian king, King Ludwig II is the inspiration behind Cinderella’s palace. Listed as a World Heritage site, with its 200 rooms and all the major technological comforts, this castle is a global symbol of the Romantic period. It’s not just a must-do for travellers with kids. Stand at Queen Mary’s bridge and get transported into a world of fairy tales of your childhood and bring alive the inner child in you!

Highline 179
a Walk in the Clouds

Next embark on a self-drive to ‘Highline 179’, the longest suspension footbridge in the world. Connecting the Ehrenberg Castle Ensemble with the remains of the 17th-century Roman Fortress Claudia, this 406-metre long, swaying bridge at a dizzying height of 114 metres offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. It gives the feeling of flying. But it is not for the faint-hearted!

Alpine Coaster and Relax @ Aquadome

Keep up the momentum by taking a ride on the Alpine coaster, one of the longest roller coasters in the world with a route length of 3.535 metres. With its steep curves, fun waves and spectacular jumps with a drop of up to 6 metres, and a 450° spin, it is perfect for a family adventure trip with passengers as young as 3 years allowed to get on

Wrap up this thrilling experience by relaxing and having a spa day at the Aquadome, the most modern thermal bath in Austria. Aquadome offers 12 pools, 7 saunas, and a 22,000 square metres area for families and children featuring cascade waterfalls, sandbanks, and a 90 metres long water slide.

Enjoy the Adrenaline Rush at Area47

If you are an adrenaline junkie, then Area47 is the perfect choice for you. With freefall and the highest ropes course in Austria, it offers an adventure of a lifetime. Some of the other activities include the Cannonball chute, blobbing activities, mountain bike tours, and many others.

Solden 007 Elements All things
James Bond

Besides adventure, Tyrol is also famous for Solden 007 Elements, a cinematic installation that provides visitors with a graphic journey into the life of James Bond, the world’s most famous spy. Through a series of interactive galleries, 007 Elements showcases the signature elements of a James Bond film – dramatic scores, jaw-dropping action sequences, gadgets and technology, and breathtaking locations and iconic studios. Standing 3,040 metres above sea level, this 1,300 square metres space is also famous as the location for the mountain action sequences of the movie ‘Spectre’.

Experience the traditions of Austria

Vienna Coffee house

Vienna is famous for its coffeehouse culture, where people gather to enjoy coffee, pastries, and conversation in elegant and historic coffeehouses.

Classical Music

Austria is renowned as the birthplace of classical music, and it has produced some of the world's greatest composers, including Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart.

Winter Sports

Austria is a winter sports paradise, and it is known for its world-class ski resorts and winter sports culture.The country has also hosted Winter Olympics.

Baroque Architecture

Austria is known for its exquisite Baroque architecture, with stunning palaces, churches, and historic buildings found throughout the country.

Christmas Markets

Austrian Christmas markets, especially those in Vienna and Salzburg, are famous for their festive atmosphere and feature handcrafted gifts, mulled wine, and traditional holiday treats.
