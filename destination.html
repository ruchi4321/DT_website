The Best of Vietnam

Rich
Cultural Heritage

Vietnam has a long history of heroic soldiers and wise monarchs. Visit Hue's Imperial City, the emperor's former residence. Visit the royal homes, temples, and gardens. Festivals in Vietnam are thrilling. Dragon dances, pyrotechnics, and parades celebrate Tet, the Lunar New Year. Join the party, try banh chung (sticky rice cake), and get fortunate red envelopes.

Vibrant
Urban Centers

Vietnam's dynamic cities blend history with modernity. Ho Chi Minh City, known as Saigon, has skyscrapers, vibrant marketplaces, and a rich history. The War Remnants Museum, Ben Thanh Market, and Nguyen Hue Walking Street include street performers. Cyclo through the Old Quarter's tiny alleyways and lively shops.

Bali is the westernmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands. A mountain range stretches from west to east of Bali - a zone of high volcanic activity. The island is famous for traditional wooden sculpture and painting, as well as batik painting on fabric.
