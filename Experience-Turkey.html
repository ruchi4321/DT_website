Thermal Hot Springs of Pamukkale

Journey to the ancient city of Pamukkale that is famous for its thermal hot water springs and white limestone travertines. Take a curative bath in Calcareous and feel the magic of this place! This UNESCO World Heritage Site, which is also known as “cotton castle”, has a natural beauty and scenic quality to it. With the ruins of the ancient city of Hierapolis nearby, Pamukkale is a hot-bed of tourists from all across the world!

The Best of Turkey

Enjoy the Spectacular Views of Cappadocia

Take a flight on the famous Cappadocia hot air balloon and witness a spectacular sunrise view while you fly over the major landmarks of the city – the fairy chimneys! Located in central Turkey, Cappadocia is known for its fairy chimneys - a group of tall, cone-shaped rock formations in Monks Valley, Göreme. The beautiful rocky valleys and legendary landscapes of Cappadocia make it one of the most popular destinations in the world for hot air ballooning. Soak up the fresh morning air while you drift through valleys scattered with pigeon houses and over orchards and vineyards. Capture these Instagram-worthy moments as you collect memories of a lifetime.

Burrow in a Cave Hotel in Goreme

Staying in a cave-like hotel is a must-do if visiting Cappadocia! Cappadocia is honeycombed with caves. Many were built by hand in the soft volcanic rocks thousands of years ago. Historically, these caves were used as homes, monasteries and churches. Now, they have been transformed into boutique hotels offering a range and quality of accommodation.

Culture Walk in the Old Town of Istanbul

The tour will take you through the Sultanahmet neighbourhood which has the cultural influence of the Roman, Byzantine and Ottoman empires. Some of the must-dos in your itinerary are the striking Blue Mosque with its iconic minarets, the mosaic-filled 6th-century Hagia Sophia which was originally a Christian church, and the Topkapi Palace, each of which is bound to leave you spell-bound! Rejuvenate yourself after the long walk and experience a unique tradition of Turkey by indulging in an authentic Turkish bath at a Hammam in Istanbul! These luxurious Hammams are carved out of caves and provide the best of both the worlds – old-world tradition in a modern setup. End your day watching the sun go down on the Golden Horn and by relishing the local foods at the numerous traditional eateries.

Party like a pro at the Beach Town of Bodrum

For all the party animals, Bodrum is the place to be! With its narrow shopping streets and beachfront restaurants famous for their sea-food, Bodrum is always bustling with tourists. But it is best known for its lively nightlife offering everything from hip-hop clubs to beach bars! Located in Southwestern Turkey, this beautiful sea-side town is known for its clear water beaches and rich history. You can enjoy a relaxed beach vacation or have an adventure-filled experience. You can go dancing at the Zorro Dancing Bar, or enjoy a quiet time at its many bars and cafes with live music. Bodrum has something for every kind of traveler!
